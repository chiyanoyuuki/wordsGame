<h2 class="main-title">Introduction</h2>
<p><span><span><em>In painting I excel, I’m playful as a fairy, and when I draw my sword I can do battle with even the Forty-seven Ronin! I am the peerless </em>ukiyo-e<em> artist, “Katsushika Hokusai!”</em></span></span></p>
<p><span><span>Even as she gets older her personality remains unchanged!<br />
Obsessed with reading, the young “Katsushika Oui” came to admire “fairies!”</span></span></p>
<p><span><span>“I’m exceptionally strong and quick-witted, the peerless <em>ukiyo-e </em>artist! And if I can have a glamorous kimono, that’d be great too!”</span></span></p>
<p><span><span>…O-Ei might be a bit too excited. (Her father is here too)</span></span></p>
<h2 class="main-title">Overview</h2>
<p><span><span><span><span><em><span>“I'll draw an' paint an' cut the opponents down at random! ...Hm? What is it, Father? "This is a dark past you'll blotch out with ink"...?”</span></em></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>The Saber incarnation of Hokusai is an Oui whose desire to be a storybook warrior rivals her desire to be an artist. Innocent and curious, if a bit immature, she is a single-target Arts damage dealer boasting high NP damage as well as good affinity towards critical damage.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Being a welfare, Hokusai’s greatest asset is her essentially guaranteed NP5, which gives her a major edge against most competitors. The NP’s own NP Damage buff further builds upon this strong offensive base, but she is able to take it one step further with the Arts Performance buff from </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Pseudonym, Dragon-Crested Octopus B</span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>. Aside from her powerful NP, Hokusai is also equipped with various critical-oriented tools, ranging from Instant Critical Stars, Star Absorption Up and even Critical Damage Up, allowing her to be a quite reliable critical damage dealer with a well-supported team. Rounding up her kit are the NP Charge from </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Divine Power (Ink) C </span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>and Guts from </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Father-Daughter Connection B, </span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>the latter of which gives her some extra leeway in terms of survivability.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Hokusai does require some external support to function properly, as her self-buffed NP refund won’t be nearly enough for an NP Spam, and despite her various critical buffs, Hokusai herself cannot produce enough stars in a consistent manner. She is also reliant on supports in terms of survivability and long-term sustain.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Nevertheless, these weaknesses don’t take away from Hokusai’s sheer damage potential, and they can be quite easily fixed by using the right supports. All in all, Hokusai can be considered a very solid Servant even at release, and with the release of </span></span></span></span></span></span><a href="https://gamepress.gg/grandorder/servant/altria-caster" rel="nofollow"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>a certain Arts support</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></a><span><span><span><span><span><span>, she might well be one of the most powerful free damage dealer options in the entire game.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h2 class="main-title">Strengths</h2>
<div id="servant-strengths">
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>High ST NP Damage</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>With all but guaranteed NP5 and two offensive buffs to provide additional damage boost, Hokusai’s NP Damage is impressive to say the least, and she can go even further with the help of proper supports. With 8 hits to its name, </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>A Tour of Waterfalls in Various Provinces </span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>is able to provide a good amount of refund, especially when external Arts Performance buffs are involved.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Critical Damage Affinity</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>With a Critical Damage buff as well as a Star Absorption buff, Hokusai is well-placed to convert available critical stars into damage. While Hokusai herself doesn’t produce a lot of stars from her Command Cards, she is able to use Divine Power (Ink)’s instant critical stars to provide an extra boost when necessary.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
</div>
<h2 class="main-title">Weaknesses</h2>
<div id="servant-weaknesses">
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Reliant on External Support</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>While Hokusai has the makings of a powerful damage dealer, both her NP refund as well as her star generation capabilities are rather limited on their own, which severely hinders her from reaching her full damage dealing potential. On the flip side, having a well-supported party will significantly boost her performance, especially if they can cover both aspects of Hokusai’s playstyle.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Lacks Long-Term Sustain</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Although Father-Daughter Connection’s Guts provides some leeway in terms of survivability, Hokusai’s lack of any sort of healing/defense buff/hard defensive skill means that she can have some survivability issues in longer fights. Fortunately, as an Arts Servant, she is generally able to find suitable defensive supports easily, making this problem a minor one in most practical cases.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
</div>

<h2 class="main-title">Profile</h2>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Character Info</h3>
<p>As was the child, so is the woman! Katsushika Oui has set out to become a fairy right out of the illustrated stories she loved in her youth!</p>
<p>"I'm an ukiyo-e artist who is both strong and smart! And this extra flashy kimono is the cherry on top!"</p>
<p>...Oei, don't you have too much going on? (Toto-sama's here, too)</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 1</h3>
<p>Height/Weight: 162cm, 51kg<br />
Origin: History<br />
Region: Japan<br />
Alignment: Chaotic-Good<br />
Gender: Female<br />
The slimy thing is Toto-sama, a bonus accessory. He followed along for some reason.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 2</h3>
<p>Oui dreamt of becoming a fairy-tale character more than an ukiyo-e artist during her youth, and this is what that girl is like all grown up.<br />
This Oui has yet to experience the obstacles of artistry and the frustrations of divorce, and she has a heady confidence in her own artistic talents that would one day surpass her own Father, Hokusai.</p>
<p>With her heart set on surpassing her father, she takes the name "Katsushika Hokusai" without permission, even though she is still a greenhorn. Even so, there's something about her that the adult Oui lacks.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 3</h3>
<p>Oui wholeheartedly believed her father's dubious tale that the Hokusai clan had familial relations with the warrior retainers of the Kira family from the famous "Chuushingura" tales, and so she developed a sense of pride as a swordswoman. On top of that, she pored over reference materials, such as literature from Takizawa Bakin and the Chinese novel "Water Margin." She let her imagination run wild, which led to her looking the way she does.</p>
<p>The legend of Katsushika Hokusai being the great-grandchild of Kobayashi Heihachirou, retainer to the tragically fated Kira Family, is unclear. And if it is true, Oui would be the great-great-granddaughter of Kobayashi Heihachirou.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 4</h3>
<p>Divine Power (Ink): C<br />
Oui is so into the idea of fairy-tale stuff that she's started brewing old Chinese medicinal drinks and trying to divine people's fortunes from their facial structure. After all, 'tis the season for everyone to get heated up and obsess over that sort of thing.</p>
<p>Father-Daughter Connection: B<br />
The incompatible thoughts between a father who watches over his naive daughter, and the daughter who wishes to surpass her father and be acknowledged.</p>
<p>Pseudonym, Dragon-Crested Octopus: B<br />
The abyssal evil god's madness. His tentacles sink into his own daughter's spirit. Perhaps their love of travelling and wandering may have caused them to change into a different Class...?</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 5</h3>
<p>[A Tour of Waterfalls in Various Provinces]<br />
Rank: B<br />
NP Type: Anti-Personnel<br />
Range: 1 - ?<br />
Maximum Target: 8</p>
<p>Shokoku Taki Meguri.<br />
An Oyo Muhen Tenkai Hishou (Heaven's Soaring Boundlessness) longsword technique that was inspired by various famous waterfalls, inviting everyone to a trip to visit waterfalls to cut through the summer heat.<br />
There are a total of eight famous waterfall prints, which were painted after the "Thirty-six Views of Mount Fuji" painting. The names of the waterfalls are:<br />
Kirifuri Waterfall on Mount Kurokami in Shimotsuke Province<br />
Kiyotaki Kannon Waterfall at Sakanoshita on the Toukaidou<br />
Aoigaoka Falls in the Eastern Capital<br />
Rouben Waterfall at Ouyama in Sagami Province<br />
The Waterfall Where Yoshitsune Washed His Horse at Yoshino in Yamato Province<br />
Yourou Waterfall in Mino Province<br />
Ono Waterfall on the Kisokaidou<br />
and The Amida Falls in the Far Reaches of the Kisokaidou Road.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 6</h3>
<p>Rather than any of his disciples, she was the one to inherit her father's penname, Katsushika Hokusai. Her wish is to enhance their fame even more, and she hopes her father will watch over her success.<br />
She knows that she is still a fledgling, but she's confident in her own creativity. As a Servant, she is brimming with curiosity, and believes a desire to learn is more important than raw talent.<br />
The tomboyish and vivacious personality is Oui herself, but at times she does find a modest, shy side of herself.</p>
</div>

  function getRank(array, stat){
    var rank = -1;
    var count = 0;
    for(var i = 0; i < array.length; i++){
      if(array[i] == stat){
        rank = i+1;
        return rank;
      }
    }
  }
  function getInfo(array, field){
    var uniques = [];
    var highest = -1;
    for(var i = 0; i < array.length; i++){
      var currVal = array[i][field];
      var currValInt = parseInt(currVal);
      var currClass = array[i].tdoll_class;
      if(currValInt > highest){
        highest = currValInt;
      }
      uniques.push(currVal);
    }
    return [uniques, highest];
  }

function sortJSONByField(json, field){
  return json.concat().sort(function(a, b){
    return parseInt(b[field]) - parseInt(a[field]);
  });
}
