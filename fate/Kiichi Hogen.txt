<h2 class="main-title">Overview</h2>
<p><span><span><span><span><em><span>Wanna join me for a little drink? What? Youre a minor? Why does that matter? Everything that lives will die one day. Live while youre alive. Laugh, cry, eat, drink! Hahah, ahahahahaha!</span></em></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>The unwilling mentor of Ushiwakamaru, and founder of the oldest school of swordsmanship in West Japan, Kiichi Hogen joins the roster as the Welfare Servant of the Little Big Tengu event. They are the second Quick AoE Assassin welfare to grace the cast, complete with a balanced offensive and supportive kit, and the ability to loop their NP.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Kiichi Hogens kit also has a multitude of offensive and supportive effects. </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Famed Blade</span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span> is their primary Card steroid and defensive option, giving them a 3-turn Quick buff and a 2-time, 3-turn Evade. The rest of their skills have the ability to support allies, with </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Onmyoudou (Buddhism) </span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>providing an ally of choice with up to 20% NP Gauge and a boost to their Star Gather Rate (which can come in handy with their ability to generate an ample number of stars via their Quick Cards and NP). Their third skill, </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Strategies of the Six Secret Teachings</span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>, provides the entire party with 3 turns of modest ATK and NP Gain buffs, making them a welcome addition to most any party.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Their NP, </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Secrets and Strategies, Great Demon's War Fan</span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>, buffs their own Quick performance before dealing damage to all enemies, and then removes defensive buffs after damage, which can come in handy for certain encounters. Due to the NPs high hit counts, it also generates a plethora of stars that can be used on the subsequent turn to deal devastating Critical Hits. Their NPs performance only further increases in the presence of Quick Support, although even without support, their NP damage among AoE Quick Assassins is on the higher end thanks to their Welfare status and ease of reaching NP5. They are capable of looping their NP with Skadi, albeit with slight difficulty (although more easily than the prior AoE Quick Assassin Welfare, Scathach (Assassin)), partially thanks to their kits NP Gain buff along with having an NP charge skill.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Overall, Kiichi Hogen is an excellent Quick AoE Assassin option for dispatching Rider waves, and true to their nature of taking on disciples to teach the way of the sword, they provide a helpful boost to any party members offensive performance. Masters in need of filling the relatively rare AoE Assassin niche wont want to miss out on recruiting Kiichi Hogen to Chaldea for both for her wave clearing capacity and her excellent supportive abilities.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h2 class="main-title">Strengths</h2>
<div id="servant-strengths">
<h3><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Welfare Status</span></span></strong></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Kiichi Hogen can be obtained for free and can easily reach NP5 by completing their event. This allows them to reach higher damage thresholds more easily, and makes them among an excellent AoE Assassin option.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><strong><span><span>NP Looping Capability</span></span></strong></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Due to their kits 20% NP charge and NP gain buffs, Kiichi Hogen is capable of looping with a double Skadi setup, although they do so with slight difficulty and are only consistent with a plugsuit setup and a third NP charge support such as Altria Caster, Waver, etc. However, they NP loop with more ease than other existing AoE Quick Assassins.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Offensive Support</span></span></strong></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Kiichi Hogen can charge an allys gauge by up to 20%, to help either top off the NP gauge of an ally, or their own. This enables immediate access to an NP in combination with a non-MLB Kaleidoscope. The charge also comes with a small Star Gather buff for the ally, which can support critical damage output. Their third skill also provides up to 20% ATK and NP Gain buffs, which help both their own damage output as well as allies.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Defensive Buff Removal on NP</span></span></strong></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Their NP removes defensive buffs after damage, which includes normal Defense Up buffs, Evade, Invincibility, Damage Cut, or any death resist buffs. Although it would be nicer if this effect happened before damage, having the ability to remove these effects on a freely available Welfare Servant is still invaluable for encounters where these buffs come into play.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
</div>
<h2 class="main-title">Weaknesses</h2>
<div id="servant-weaknesses">
<h3><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Low Class Damage Multiplier</span></span></strong></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>The Assassin damage multiplier of x0.8 doesnt do Kiichi Hogen any favors, and although this affects all Assassins across the board, the demerit is notable enough to likely pigeonhole Kiichi Hogen into the role of a Rider (or Berserker) wave farmer, where they can benefit fully from Class advantage. This damage multiplier makes Kiichi Hogens damage sometimes not quite reach the threshold required for farming waves without support, despite their NP5 welfare status.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Mediocre Card NP Gain</span></span></strong></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Kiichi Hogens NP Gain outside of looping setups is somewhat mediocre, being a little below average for her class. In most cases, it can be tough to charge her NP gauge quickly if her own charge skill is on cooldown, at least without support or crits in play. She is somewhat reliant on the presence of her single Arts Card for high Card NP gain turns.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
</div>

<h2 class="main-title">Profile</h2>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Character Info</h3>
<p>An onmyouji priest and strategist, she is described in legend as the founder of the Kyouhachiryu, the oldest school of swordsmanship in western Japan. She taught young Ushiwakamaru the art of war and even passed on the most closely guarded secrets of warfare, the "Six Secret Teachings" and the "Three Strategies of Huang Shigong"...though not on purpose. Kiichi Hogen became murderously angry after learning that Ushiwakamaru tried to seduce her daughter to sneak a look at the manuscripts, making her a truly terrifying mentor to Ushiwakamaru.</p>
<p>Her name: Kiichi Hogen.<br />
But her true identity is...?</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 1</h3>
<p>Height/Weight: 171cm, 57kg<br />
Origin: "Gikeiki," Otogizoushi, Legend of the Kurama Tengu, etc.<br />
Region: Japan<br />
Alignment: Chaotic-Neutral<br />
Gender: - (Current physical body is female)<br />
She can change her appearance and gender at will. Her current form is her most frequently used one, but apparently her most impressive appearance is different.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 2</h3>
<p>Famed Blade: A+<br />
Kiichi Hogen is said to be the founder of the legendary school of swordsmanship "Kyouhachiryu." Kyouhachiryu is considered to be the origin of Western Japanese swordsmanship, consisting of the eight styles Kiichi taught her eight leading disciples. One of the disciples is said to have been Minamoto-no-Yoshitsune (Ushiwakamaru).</p>
<p>Onmyoudou (Buddhism): A<br />
Kiichi Hogen was an influential Buddhist onmyouji in the capital during the late days of the Heian Era.</p>
<p>Strategies of the Six Secret Teachings: EX<br />
The ultimate collection of strategies, regarded as one of the world's greatest. It is a legendary strategic book on par with the "Three Strategies," and is considered to be one of the Seven Military Classics in China.</p>
<p>Kiichi Hogen is the guardian of this manuscript.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 3</h3>
<p>She is happy-go-lucky. She does not worry about the little things, laughing them off without a care in the world. At first glance, her words and actions speak of an inclusive, humane, and reliable nature... But in reality, her sensibilities are far removed from anything human, and even a brief conversation with her makes clear that she's a little...off in relation to humans.</p>
<p>She's basically just not human.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 4</h3>
<p>The supernatural entity named Kiichi Hogen is often identified with the Kurama Tengu, or the great tengu Soujoubou (the high priest and god of the tengu) of Mt. Kurama. The Edo period joururi (a Japanese dramatic narrative) titled Kiichi Hogen Sanryaku-no-maki lists Kiichi Hogen as the alias of Soujoubou, a tengu from Mt. Kurama.</p>
<p>In this game, too, Kiichi Hogen is not considered human. She is the great tengu.</p>
<p>She is an Elemental who aligns with the mountains, sky, wind, fire, and other natural aspects, and while she is not a Divine Spirit, she has more in common with them than with humans.</p>
<p>Although she does not normally interact with humans, there have been some examples of her bestowing something upon humans, as in the case of Ushiwakamaru and the Kyouhachiryu. Her gifts tend to be knowledge, skills, and secret techniques...though not exclusively.</p>
<p>The moment you put her in a bad mood, you are liable to be sent (quite literally) to hell. After all, a great tengu as renowned as the Kurama Soujoubou should have no difficulty opening the gates of hell.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 5</h3>
<p>[Secrets and Strategies, Great Demon's War Fan]<br />
Rank: A<br />
NP Type: Anti-Fortress<br />
Range: 1 - 50<br />
Maximum Targets: 500</p>
<p>Rikutou Sanryaku, Maou Sondaiusen.<br />
The usen refers to a fan made from the feathers of a hawk or eagle. It is a tool used to command troops, but in other instances, they also appear in legends and paintings as mystic Noble Phantasms possessing enormous divine power used by sages and tengu.</p>
<p>It is a tengu's fan, and Assassin Kiichi Hogen uses one of these fans (feathered uchiwa) for her Noble Phantasm.</p>
<p>Its destructive power is, in a word, tremendous. This Noble Phantasm better embodies the properties of Kurama Tengu, the great tengu known as Soujoubou of Mt. Kurama, as opposed to Kiichi Hogen, the onmyouji and strategist. Upon its True Name revelation, the power of its winds is on the level of a storm or a cataclysm that splits the mountains and shakes the earth.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 6</h3>
<p>She is not as knowledgeable about the workings of the human mind. However, sometimes, when she is in the mood, she has been known to grant the prayers of the weak, or raise and instruct individuals who have hidden talent like Ushiwakamaru. She does not understand humans that well, but she doesnt hate them, in the same way that she does not hate animals or insects.</p>
<p>She loves parties.<br />
Her signature laugh is kanra kara kara!</p>
</div>

  function getRank(array, stat){
    var rank = -1;
    var count = 0;
    for(var i = 0; i < array.length; i++){
      if(array[i] == stat){
        rank = i+1;
        return rank;
      }

  function getInfo(array, field){
    var uniques = [];
    var highest = -1;
    for(var i = 0; i < array.length; i++){
      var currVal = array[i][field];
      var currValInt = parseInt(currVal);
      var currClass = array[i].tdoll_class;
      if(currValInt > highest){
        highest = currValInt;
      }
      uniques.push(currVal);
    }
    return [uniques, highest];
  }
