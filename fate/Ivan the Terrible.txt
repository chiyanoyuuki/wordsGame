<h2 class="main-title">Overview</h2>
<p><span><span><span><span><em><span>Time for worshipping is finished. Therefore... Next, it's the time to battle.</span></em></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>A fusion between the human known as Ivan the Terrible and an unknown Demonic Beast, the Lightning Emperor Ivan rules over the Eternally Frozen Empire - Anastasia with equal measures of cruelty and piety. A Rider-class damage dealer Servant with Buster-type AoE NP with a straightforward but effective kit.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>As a damage dealer, much of Ivans capability to deal damage rests on the various buffs granted by his NP </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Zveri Krestnyi Khod </span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>and second skill </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Innocent Monster (Foreign). </span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>The two Buster Performance boosts they provide are particularly notable, as both last for 3 turns, giving Ivan a considerable lead in terms of sustained damage over his competitors. Furthermore, his skills also enhance his NP Gain (</span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Mental Schism</span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>) and provide some amount of critical stars (Innocent Monster (Foreign)), helping him unleash more NPs as well as critical hits, the latter of which is also aided by his naturally high Star Weight. Outside of the damage department, Ivan rounds up his kit with an Invincibility buff, as well as two utility tools in the form of self-debuff removal and buff removal (for all enemies).</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>The main weakness Ivans Buster-centric kit, however, is that it lacks any sort buff capable of boosting his other cards, which lowers his damage outside of burst turns. Furthermore, he also lacks a tool integral to AoE Servants, which is an NP Charge skill. This results in an almost complete dependency on supports to perform instant NPs, which makes him a less desirable option for farming. His last and least significant weakness is the fact that a lot of his skills effects often have different usage timings, which, sometimes, makes using them (</span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Emergency Prerogative </span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>in particular) a bit of a dilemma.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>All said, however, Ivan the Terrible is a highly solid AoE Rider option by all metrics, whose Buster cards are sure to deliver heavy blows to his opponents. While his lack of NP Charge lessens his potential as a farming unit, the damage and utility the Emperor of Lightning provides are more than enough to make up for it. </span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h2 class="main-title">Strengths</h2>
<div id="servant-strengths">
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Three-Turn Buster Performance Boosts</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>The long durations of Ivans offensive buffs ensure that he would be able to get the most out of it, even when his Buster cards are unavailable during the NP Brave chain turn. Furthermore, the Buster Performance boost provided by his NP comes in the form of a debuff on all enemies, which benefits the Emperors allies as well as himself.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Good NP Gain and Critical Compatibility</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Aside from having access to a considerable NP Gain buff, Ivans naturally high Star Weight enables him to benefit doubly through his reliable critical hits, which also provide an increase to his overall damage. To further improve on his critical capability, Ivans second skill provides a decent amount of critical stars every turn, stars which, while not enough to ensure criticals on their own, allow for more flexibility for supports as well as their CE choices.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Good Utility</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Ivans AoE buff removal on enemies is generally considered to be both rare as well as highly valuable, and this gives him a significant edge over his competitors in terms of flexibility for difficult quests.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
</div>
<h2 class="main-title">Weaknesses</h2>
<div id="servant-weaknesses">
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Lacks non Card-Specific Damage Boost</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Ivans various three-turns Buster performance boosts, on first glance, may seem to be a significant increase to his sustained damage output, but in reality, it is balanced out by the fact that he lacks any sort of neutral damage battery (i.e. Attack Up or Critical Damage Up). While this is less of a problem during NP Brave turns (where Buster cards generally excel), the NP charging properties of Arts and Quick cards are generally preferable outside of it, which reduces the significance of his buffs three-turn durations. Fortunately, proper use of damage supports is generally more than enough to make this weakness largely unnoticeable.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>No NP Charge Skill</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Some extent of farming capability is generally expected of AoE Servants, but it is one of the fields Ivan doesnt particularly excel at. While his above average NP damage allows him to kill higher-tier mobs more reliably, his lack of instant NP Charge places a restriction on his CE choices, and may force him to rely on supports to unleash early-turn NPs.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Conflicting Skill Effects</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>While the various effects contained within Ivans skills are undoubtedly powerful, their usage timing often conflicts with each other, which means that hes often unable to gain their full benefits. This aspect of his kit also presents a dilemma in more difficult quests, as Masters will often be forced to choose to forego one effect or the other.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
</div>

    var highest = -1;
    for(var i = 0; i < array.length; i++){

      var currValInt = parseInt(currVal);
      var currClass = array[i].tdoll_class;

      }
      uniques.push(currVal);
