<h2 class="main-title">Overview</h2>
<p><span><span><span><span><em><span>I dont feel like changing. I mean, Xiang Yu complimented this swimsuit.</span></em></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>When Xiang Yu told Yu Meiren to get in a bathing suit and enjoy summer, her true nature emerged even if shes a bit tsundere about it. As a </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Single Target Quick Lancer</span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>, not only did Yu transform from an Assassin to a Lancer, she transformed from one of the worst Servants in the game to one of the premiere male boss killers around.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Yus kit is packed to the gills with all one needs to kill a man. Her burst damage is insane thanks to </span></span></span></span></span></span><strong>Dance of Bygone Days</strong><span><span><span><strong><span><span> EXs</span></span></strong><span><span><span> ability to both boost her Quick and the partys Critical damage. Combined with her Supereffective modifier on her Noble Phantasm against men, theres no chance. With the right supports, she also has great sustained damage as well, having high crits for several turns. And of course, she has the right self-sufficiencies with a charge on </span></span></span></span></span></span><strong>Conqueror's Princess EX</strong><span><span><span><span><span><span><strong> </strong>and a Guts on </span></span></span><strong><span><span>Summer Incarnated Elemental A+</span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>The main problem with Yu comes from her sustainability. While she does have a Guts skill to survive one time, that is counteracted by how shell receive all hits the turn she uses her </span></span></span></span></span></span>Conqueror's Princess<span><span><span><span><span><span> skill. Shell also have a bit of problem with NP gain without support even with her boosts. Itll also be a challenge getting stars to her as she has minimal generation.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Yu is used by Masters as a boss killer, capable of doing close to a million damage on her Noble Phantasm with the proper setup against Male Archers. The only real question is whether she really did change her clothes because Xiang Yu told her to, or if she just was tired of being a bottom of the barrel Assassin.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h2 class="main-title">Strengths</h2>
<div id="servant-strengths">
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Burst Damage</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Yu has so much going for her with Burst Damage that she competes with even some top tier boss killers like Scathach for one-hit strength. Her Noble Phantasm alone is Quick type which makes it slightly more powerful than others, but comes with a 50% bonus against Males. As this is tied to her Noble Phantasm, it applies multiplicatively with Craft Essence boosts such as NP Damage up or event damage bonuses. If that werent enough, she gets a huge chunk of damage boost from her Quick crits, dealing an extra 50% for every Crit. A NPQQ chain in theory could deal close to 1.5 million damage with a Black Grail and a double Skadi support.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Sustained Damage</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Yus biggest buffs to her Crit and Quick last 3-turns on a 5-turn cooldown, so she will not be long without them in any extended fight. As these are sizeable boosts, she is able to deal damage throughout the fight if needed.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Self-Sufficient</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Although supports help her immensely, Yu has what all Servants need to operate independently. For the NP side of things she has a charge and NP gain rate buffs (which can increase on certain fields types). And for survival, she has a pretty heft guts reviving her with about half health. </span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
</div>
<h2 class="main-title">Weaknesses</h2>
<div id="servant-weaknesses">
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Low Durability</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Yu does have a Guts to survive, but this may in effect be cutting her health in half from the start. Her charge skill brings the attention of all enemies to her for a turn, and without an evasion or invincibility shell be taking that as damage. Seeing how shes likely facing bosses, this could be enough to knock her main first HP bar out.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Low Natural NP Gain</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Yu has a relatively high NP gain modifier at 1.1% per hit, but her cards hit counts are rather low with only a 3-hit Quick and 4-hit Extra. While this is boosted some by Summer Incarnated Elemental, shell likely have to rely on supports for the rest of her gauge.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Low Star Gain</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>As a Single-target Lancer, Yu has difficulty producing her own stars unlike some of the other Quick Lancers. Her lower hit counts make it hard to produce a stash of stars, and her supports likely will not be providing much either for her without CEs. </span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
</div>

<h2 class="main-title">Profile</h2>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Character Info</h3>
<p>"The summer resort! A vacation with my Xiang Yu!"</p>
<p>And with a flash, Yu Mei-ren happily transformed her Spirit Origin into a swimsuit, but...things happened and Xiang Yu was left behind in Chaldea.<br />
- End of chapter 1 -</p>
<p>However...<br />
Even though Yu Mei-ren's dreams were shattered, the investigation of the Singularity was far from over. General Xiang Yu's calculations indicate the presence of summer temptations, and so the gorgeous lady of the ages takes up the spear loaned to her and steps out into the summer Singularity.<br />
- End of chapter 2 -</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 1</h3>
<p>Height/Weight: 160cm, 49kg<br />
Origin: History, The Book of Han<br />
Region: China<br />
Alignment: Lawful-Evil<br />
Gender: Female<br />
She will do anything to maintain order between her and Xiang Yu.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 2</h3>
<p>Yu Mei-ren learns that the "beast of summer temptation" that Xiang Yu mentioned referred to "delusions over the summer" and "decreased ability to resist seduction."</p>
<p>Rather than be concerned, Yu Mei-ren says, "does that mean...my Xiang Yu is concerned that I will be seduced by others!?" As she becomes more enamored with Xiang Yu, she also became more relentless than ever about turning away any males who approach her.<br />
End of Yu Mei-ren's Summer </p>
<p>Personality:<br />
She is essentially the same as the normal Yu Mei-ren, but after borrowing Xiang Yu's spear, she is in slightly higher spirits. At the same time, she feels an even closer bond with Xiang Yu, and while her general distaste for others is still the same, she has become even more unforgiving of any who hit on her. Flirts must die.</p>
<p>This applies not only to those who try to pick her up, but also to anybody who looks at her funny (even if she only thinks it happened). As such, her spear will surely seek out anyone who so much as glances at her swimsuit form. A necessary move to protect my Xiang Yu's chastity, is her claim.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 3</h3>
<p>Summer Incarnated Elemental: A+<br />
The fact remains the same she is still an incarnated elemental. But...with her Spirit Origin changing into a swimsuit form, she is slightly more optimized for summer. Her absorption efficiency increases if she is surrounded by summer-themed nature, like the water from a lake in a campground or bright sunlight. One may think this is contrary to how a vampire ought to behave, but she clearly doesn't care. And because her preferences are unchanged, you may see her out sunbathing, complaining how hot it is.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 4</h3>
<p>Dance of Bygone Days: EX<br />
A skill that represents the sword dance that was once performed for Xiang Yu. She may detest human culture and think that mimicking it is absurd, but she may also bend that rule for her Xiang Yu.</p>
<p>She doesn't know if her dance is good. She doesn't know how others perceive her performance, but it doesn't matter. The only thing she knows is that at times, dancing was necessary. And she is the only one who knows how Xiang Yu assessed her dance and what his face looked like as he watched her. The sight and skill have been deeply engraved in her own Spirit Origin as a precious memory.</p>
<p>Normally this skill is sealed or unusable unless Xiang Yu is nearby. But this time around, the impact of Xiang Yu's spear being loaned to her broke the skill's seal, enabling it to be used with the spear serving as a king of substitute. It has now been arranged to be a spear dance.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--servant-profile-entry paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h3 class="sub-title" style="margin-top:8px">Profile 5</h3>
<p>[Anti-Fling Rondo]<br />
Rank: A<br />
NP Type: Anti-Army<br />
Range: 1 - 10<br />
Maximum Targets: 20</p>
<p>The Lance Dance To Annihilate Summer Monsters.<br />
The term "fling" in this case refers to "unfaithfulness" or "summer love," and it indirectly points to the summer monster mentioned throughout this event. The loaned spear is an embodiment of Xiang Yu's love (at least she thinks it is), and is used as an anti-summer monster weapon that eradicates the enemy. It also has a special attack against males due to her outrageous presumptions.</p>
<p>Though Yu Mei-ren never trained in martial arts or tactics, her love for Xiang Yu was roused to the extreme, and she remembered the sword dance she once performed for him. She figured she could leverage that same maneuver but using a spear, which resulted in this Noble Phantasm. For some reason her dance turned out to resemble pole dancing.</p>
<p>Much like with her original Noble Phantasm "Eternal Lament," she destroys her own physical body to make her magical energy go out of control to induce calamitous weather. But as she spins using Xiang Yu's spear as her axis, the rotation creates a tornado-shaped curse instead of a curse of bloody rain. The eternal lament has changed form to become the rondo dance of death.</p>
</div>

  function getRank(array, stat){
    var rank = -1;
    var count = 0;
    for(var i = 0; i < array.length; i++){
      if(array[i] == stat){
        rank = i+1;
        return rank;
      }
    }
  }
  function getInfo(array, field){
    var uniques = [];
    var highest = -1;
    for(var i = 0; i < array.length; i++){
      var currVal = array[i][field];
      var currValInt = parseInt(currVal);
      var currClass = array[i].tdoll_class;
      if(currValInt > highest){
        highest = currValInt;
      }
      uniques.push(currVal);
    }
    return [uniques, highest];
  }

function sortJSONByField(json, field){
  return json.concat().sort(function(a, b){
    return parseInt(b[field]) - parseInt(a[field]);
  });
}
