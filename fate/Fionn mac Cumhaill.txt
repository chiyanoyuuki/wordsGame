<h2 class="main-title">Overview</h2>
<p><span><span><span><span><em><span>I see. So this is how I gain more power.</span></em></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Fionn used to have a reputation for being a really bad Servant, to the point where some would jokingly call him a Rare Prism. Fortunately, a set of well-placed buffs has brought Fionn back to a position of strength. While hes not the best at rapid damage output, Fionn has a lot of defense and utility tools that can help his team survive for long periods of time, making him a good choice for difficult fights. He also has the potential to loop his Noble Phantasm with proper support, making him a good Archer-farming Servant once Altria Caster is available.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>With all his buffs in play, Fionns primary claim to fame is his defensive value. </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Uninhibited Resplendence</span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span> lets Fionn protect his entire party from single-target attacks for a turn, while </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Mac an Luin </span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>reduces the enemy teams attack, keeping enemy damage under control. Fionn also has </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Clairvoyance (Beauty) </span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>to speed up the whole team by increasing the partys Critical Star Drop Rate and NP Gain. Additionally, while Fionns normal attack NP gain is weak, his Noble Phantasm has strong NP gain, meaning he can use his NP multiple turns in a row under the right circumstances.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Fionn does have several notable weaknesses, however. As is typical of Servants with lots of utility tools, Fionns damage is quite low, as his only innate damage boost lasts just one turn. Fionn also has very low NP gain on his normal attacks, which means he will struggle to gain access to his NP without the help of support Servants. Furthermore, he has fairly long cooldowns, which means he has to wait for several turns after using his skills before he can benefit from them a second time.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Fionn has come a long way. Where he used to be one of the worst SR Servants in the game, Fionn now has a clear place as a defense-oriented Arts unit. What he lacks in damage, he makes up for in his ability to keep his team alive and moving. For difficult fights where survivability is the name of the game, Fionn is a strong damage-dealer.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h2 class="main-title">Strengths</h2>
<div id="servant-strengths">
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Defensive Support</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Fionns Target Focus and Evade combination is a powerful form of defensive support, as it allows Fionn to protect his whole team from single-target damage for a turn. This can allow Fionn to protect his team against either single-target Noble Phantasms or dangerous critical attacks, both of which can potentially end an otherwise-successful fight. Fionns NP also reduces enemy attack, providing a bit of additional protection.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Party Utility</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Clairvoyance (Beauty) boosts the entire partys Critical Star Drop Rate and NP Gain. Not only does this help Fionn himself gain access to his NP, but it also helps support Servants use their NPs more frequently, which is important for defense-oriented teams.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>NP Loop Potential</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Fionns NP gain on his NP is high enough that, with the help of supports, he can use his NP multiple times in a row. Altria Caster, in particular, makes Fionn a viable farming Servant, especially against Archer enemies.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
</div>
<h2 class="main-title">Weaknesses</h2>
<div id="servant-weaknesses">
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Low Damage Output</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>While Fionn benefits from an NP Interlude, his only damage buff is both weak and short-lived. As a result, he may struggle to deal enough damage for Challenge Quests and farming, especially at lower NP levels.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Low Normal Attack NP Gain</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Fionns normal attacks have unusually low NP gain. While Clairvoyance (Beauty) offsets this a little bit, Fionn still struggles to build his NP gauge without the help of NP charge skills and supports. He may have trouble building towards his first NP if he does not have a Starting NP Gauge CE.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<h3><span><span><span><span><span><span>Long Skill Cooldowns</span></span></span></span></span></span></h3>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Fionns skills have generally long cooldowns, especially relative to their effect durations. Fionns longest-lasting skill, Clairvoyance (Beauty), is active about half the time at max level, while his other skills are active 1 turn out of 7 and 1 turn out of 5. This means Fionn has to be careful with the timing of his skills, as he will have to wait several turns before he can benefit from the effects of his skills again.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
</div>

The dodge can fail at levels below 10, the NP charge effect scales with level, and cooldown times on defensive skills can be the difference between a win and a loss in a hard fight, all of which make this skill a high priority for leveling. </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Clairvoyance (Beauty)</span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span> is a significant performance increase for Fionn and his allies and should be leveled second. </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Magecraft </span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>increases Fionns potential damage output, but the short duration makes it less appealing than Fionns other skills, so it can be saved for last. <li><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Uninhibited Resplendence </span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>draws enemy attacks to Fionn while also protecting him from those attacks. In difficult fights, this skill should be saved for turns where enemies are likely to use powerful critical attacks or single-target NPs. Taking enemy hits also makes it easier for Fionn to gain access to his Noble Phantasm, as he benefits from defensive NP gain on the attacks he takes. The skill also provides a flat NP charge, which makes this skill useful for farming.</span></span></span></span></span></span></li>
<li><span><span><span><strong><span><span>Magecraft </span></span></strong></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span>is Fionns only damage buff. Arts buffs are especially good as card-type buffs go, as they increase both damage and effective NP gain. Unfortunately, this only lasts for a single turn, and the buff is weaker than most one-turn damage boosts, making this an underwhelming skill in practice.</span></span></span></span></span></span></li>
</ul>

  function getRank(array, stat){
    var rank = -1;
    var count = 0;
    for(var i = 0; i < array.length; i++){
      if(array[i] == stat){
        rank = i+1;
        return rank;
      }
    }
  }
  function getInfo(array, field){
    var uniques = [];
    var highest = -1;
    for(var i = 0; i < array.length; i++){
      var currVal = array[i][field];
      var currValInt = parseInt(currVal);
      var currClass = array[i].tdoll_class;
      if(currValInt > highest){
        highest = currValInt;
      }
      uniques.push(currVal);
    }
    return [uniques, highest];
  }

function sortJSONByField(json, field){
  return json.concat().sort(function(a, b){
    return parseInt(b[field]) - parseInt(a[field]);
  });
}
